About Touque & Toucan

I'm a Brazilian who made the journey north to Canada, trading humid rainforests and vibrant market stalls overflowing with exotic ingredients for a different kind of wilderness: vast, cold, and equally untamed.

Touque & Toucan is my attempt at combining two homes into one, and my way of turning that rabbit hole (or should I say armadillo hole?) into something real. A little bit of the tropics, a little bit of the great white north.
