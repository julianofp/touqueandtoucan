About Touque & Toucan

I'm a Brazilian who made the journey north to Canada, bringing with me memories of humid rainforests, vibrant markets filled with exotic botanicals, and the rugged spirit of my homeland. When I arrived in the Great White North, I found a different kind of wilderness - vast, cold, and equally untamed.

Growing up, I was captivated by those classic adventure films - the ones where explorers hacked through dense jungle undergrowth, discovered hidden waterfalls, and navigated uncharted territories with nothing but their wits and determination. There's something about that old-school sense of adventure, that raw connection to wild places, that's always stayed with me.

Touque & Toucan is my way of bringing these two worlds together. I'm crafting grooming products that blend Brazilian ingredients I grew up with - the plants, oils, and scents of the South American wilderness - with the precision and quality standards I've learned in Canada. It's a personal project, a bridge between two countries I call home, inspired by a love for adventure and the untamed beauty of nature.
